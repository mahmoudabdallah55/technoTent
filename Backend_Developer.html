Backend Developer.

A Backend Developer plays a critical role in software development by building and maintaining the server-side logic, databases, and integration of APIs that power the application. Their work ensures that the software functions as intended and efficiently handles user requests, data management, and business logic.

  • Handle Authentication and Authorization

  • Test the Backend
  • Optimize Performance
  • Secure the Backend
  • Deploy the Backend
  • Maintain and Iterate
